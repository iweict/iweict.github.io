Call for Paper

2023 International Workshop on Emerging ICT is an annual conference and aims at a good mutual understanding of educational and research activities of the three universities (Tohoku U, Yeungnam U and DLUT). Undergraduate students and graduate students gather to present their university lives and research topics in the area of electronics and communications. The first conference was held at Yeungnam University in Nov. 2004. This year's conference will be held on-site and online, from 30 to 31 Oct. 2023 .

Topics of interest include, but are not limited to:

  • High-speed communication systems
  • Wireless communication
  • Optical communication
  • Signal processing
  • Image processing
  • Speech and audio processing
  • User interface
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Natural Language Processing
  • Medical application
  • Intelligent transportation system
  • Intelligent robotics
  • Wireless power tranfer
  • Measurement by electromagnetic wave
  • Intelligent hardware
  • Cyber security and hardware security

In addition to ordinary research papers, we welcome survey presentations by undergraduate students. We are planning to organize a special session of short presentations by undergraduate students (Young Researcher's Session).
