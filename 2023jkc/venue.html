Conference Venue

The workshop will be held at the Future Hall, Aobayama Campus, Tohoku University.

The Future Hall is a modern and spacious building that hosts various academic and cultural events. It is located on the Aobayama Campus, which is home to many faculties and research institutes of Tohoku University.

Tohoku University is one of the leading national universities in Japan, with a history of over 100 years. It is located in Sendai City, Miyagi Prefecture, which is known as the "City of Trees". Sendai is a vibrant and beautiful city with rich culture and nature.
