Instruction for oral presentation

  • One presentation has 15 minutes including discussion. Please make sure your computer can make presentation properly with the projector before the session begins.

  • We prepare a projector with both VGA and HDMI interfaces. Please bring your own computer to make a presentation. If you need another equipment for presentation, please contact the conference chair beforehand.

Instruction for poster presentation:

  • The poster size should be no larger than A0 size (841 x 1189 mm).

  • We prepare stickers. Please use stickers to adhere the poster on the designated poster panel.
